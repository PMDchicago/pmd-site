About Us

Prairie Management & Development, Inc. (PMD) is a mission-driven real estate development and property management company headquartered in Chicago, Illinois. Our company has over 40 years of industry experience and is dedicated to creating clean, safe and affordable housing throughout the Midwest. In addition to residential communities, we develop and operate commercial and self‑storage facilities. The company’s headquarters is situated just steps from the site of the Old Fort Dearborn.

Guided by integrity and transparency, PMD strives to build thriving communities that enrich the lives of our residents, provide rewarding opportunities for our employees and deliver value for our investors. Every project is approached with a long-term vision, ensuring that our properties foster a sense of belonging and contribute positively to their neighborhoods.

Our leadership team brings together decades of experience across development, property management and community engagement. We value collaboration and continuous improvement and remain focused on our mission of delivering exceptional experiences for those we serve.
